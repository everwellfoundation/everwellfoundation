About

Built on the belief that
how we live matters.

Everwell began with a simple question — if we had the means to do good, what would that actually look like in practice?

We are two people who have been fortunate. Not in an extraordinary way, but in the accumulating, quiet way that most good fortune arrives — through work, through each other, through circumstance. And after a while, fortune starts to feel less like something you keep and more like something you pass on.

The foundation is our attempt at that. A structure for giving that is thoughtful rather than impulsive, sustained rather than occasional. We do not seek recognition. We seek impact — small, real, and lasting.

The journal is the rest of it. The travels, the meals, the mornings that moved us, the books that changed how we thought about something. A life being lived alongside the work, not in spite of it.
